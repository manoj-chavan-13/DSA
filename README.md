## 🛠️ How I Approach Each Problem
For every DSA question you find in this repository, my aim is not just to "solve" it, but to *master* it. Here is the format you will find for every problem I tackle:

### 1. 🔗 The Question
Every problem folder will contain a direct link to the original problem statement (whether it is on LeetCode, GeeksforGeeks, Codeforces, HackerRank, etc.).

### 2. 🧠 The Thought Process (Intuition)
I will write down exactly how I thought about the problem. I will document where I got stuck, what hints helped me, and the core intuition behind the final working logic.

### 3. 🪜 Multiple Solutions (From Worst to Best)
Interviews are not just about getting the right answer; they are about optimization. Therefore, I will document multiple ways I can solve the question and walk through the progression:
- **Approach 1: Brute Force** - The naive approach to just get it working.
- **Approach 2: Better Strategy** - Applying optimizations or data structures to improve time or space constraints.
- **Approach 3: Optimal Solution** - The most efficient algorithm possible.

*For every approach, I will clearly state the Time Complexity **O(T)** and Space Complexity **O(S)**.*
